\vspace*{2em}
I am writing to join Corti's Talent Community. I am a curious data scientist with seven years of experience in applied machine learning, medical imaging, pharmaceutical analytics, and ML engineering. My work at RaySearch Laboratories is directly relevant to Corti's mission. There, I worked on automating radiotherapy planning, helping to improve patient outcomes whilst saving clinicians' time. I developed methods using autoencoders, sparse Gaussian processes, and optimisation on segmented 3D CT scans, and collaborated with medical physicists and clinicians to align the models with oncological standards and practical workflows. That experience made the gap between a working model and a clinically useful model very concrete. Working at RaySearch was genuinely exciting and I would like to get the chance to work in a similar domain again.

\hspace*{2em}
I have also worked on applied ML systems in pharmaceutical and industrial settings. At Signum Life Science, I am building an end-to-end forecasting platform for pharmaceutical price and demand. Earlier in the role, I owned and extended a next-best-action system for pharmaceutical sales, including explainable AI features built in collaboration with UX designers. At Valcon, I led development of a production real-time neural-network pipeline involving natural-language inputs and high-dimensional labels, while also mentoring colleagues and running explainable AI training.

\hspace*{2em}
Though I do not have direct experience with clinical dialogue systems, my background is highly relevant: Healthcare AI, pharmaceutical analytics, production ML pipelines, explainability, and working with domain experts when model behaviour matters.
