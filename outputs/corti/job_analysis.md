# Job Analysis: Join Corti's Talent Community

## Listing Summary

- **Company:** Corti
- **Listing:** Join Corti's Talent Community
- **Nature of listing:** General talent-community application rather than a single role.
- **Stated mission:** Expand access to medical expertise through AI.
- **Stated product focus:** Clinical-grade AI, models and infrastructure for healthcare software, clinical and administrative workflows, and real patient interactions.
- **Named model:** Symphony.
- **Named customer signal:** Corti serves over 100 million patients annually, including the NHS.

## Main Signals From The Listing

- Healthcare AI and medical expertise.
- Clinical-grade AI rather than generic ML.
- Reliable AI built on a strong foundation.
- Quality dialogue in healthcare.
- Models trained and validated on real clinical data from patient interactions.
- Infrastructure for EHR vendors, virtual care platforms, health systems, startups, and enterprises.
- Broad openness to AI technology, machine learning, and software development backgrounds.
- Collaborative work with domain experts and innovators.

## Explicit Qualification Signals

The listing does not provide a formal requirements list. The clearest implied signals are:

- Interest in healthcare AI and medical technology.
- Machine learning, AI technology, or software development skills.
- Ability to contribute to models, infrastructure, or data-driven healthcare products.
- Motivation to work on practical healthcare impact.
- Openness to candidates whose experience is close but not exact.

## Tailoring Strategy

The materials should centre on:

- Direct healthcare AI evidence from RaySearch.
- Pharmaceutical analytics and product-oriented ML from Signum.
- Production ML and natural-language-input exposure from Valcon.
- Explainable AI and stakeholder/domain-expert collaboration.
- A modest statement of interest in Corti's clinical-grade AI work.

The materials should not claim:

- Direct work on clinical dialogue systems.
- Experience with EHR systems or virtual care platforms.
- Direct experience with Corti, Symphony, NHS deployments, or Corti's clinical datasets.
- Publications, grades beyond those in the profile, or unsupported tools.
