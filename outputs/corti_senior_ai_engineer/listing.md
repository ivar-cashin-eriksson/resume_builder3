## Company Context

Corti's stated mission is to ensure everyone has access to medical expertise, no matter where they are. The listing frames Corti as a clinical-grade AI company building models and infrastructure for healthcare software, including EHR vendors, virtual care platforms, and health systems. The listing says Corti serves over 100 million patients annually, including the NHS, and that its flagship model, Symphony, is trained and validated on real clinical data from patient interactions.

## Role Summary

The role is a hands-on Senior AI Engineer position focused on agentic frameworks. The engineer will build LLM-powered systems and agentic workflows for core Corti product capabilities, with emphasis on validation practices, low-latency inference services, and dependable real-world production behaviour.

The role sits at the intersection of AI, product, and engineering, using large language models, retrieval architectures, and context-aware agents to solve real-world problems.

## Main Responsibilities

- Design and build LLM-powered product features used in production.
- Develop agentic workflows and frameworks that coordinate multiple AI components.
- Implement RAG architectures using embeddings and vector search.
- Build systems for prompting, context engineering, and tool usage.
- Develop evaluation frameworks to measure LLM and agent performance.
- Work with product and platform teams to turn AI capabilities into reliable, scalable product features.
- Improve reliability, latency, and cost efficiency of AI pipelines.

## Requirements And Signals

- Strong Python and production-grade coding ability.
- LLM experience in at least some of: transformer training, fine-tuning or post-training; production LLM inference services; embeddings, vector databases, and semantic search; RAG implementation; robust evaluations for agent workflows and generative systems.
- Production-grade ML systems experience, including deployable and operable systems, pipelines, CI/CD, and monitoring.
- Product mindset and ability to translate ideas into working systems.
- Communication across research, engineering, and product.
- Master's degree in computer science, engineering, mathematics, statistics, physics, or related field, or equivalent experience.
- Nice to have: healthcare data, clinical NLP, privacy and safety considerations, and Go.
