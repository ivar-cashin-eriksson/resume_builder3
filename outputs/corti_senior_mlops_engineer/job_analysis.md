## Tailoring Strategy

The application should position Ivar as an applied ML engineer and data scientist who has repeatedly owned the difficult middle ground between models and usable products. The strongest honest fit is:

- end-to-end ML pipeline ownership;
- healthcare and pharmaceutical domain experience;
- production ML delivery;
- Python, MLflow, Docker, Databricks, AWS, and Azure exposure;
- model explainability and domain-user collaboration;
- mentoring, training, and stakeholder communication.

The application should not overclaim pure DevOps or platform-engineering depth. The gap report should explicitly flag missing evidence for Kubernetes, Terraform, Helm, ArgoCD, Kubeflow, DVC, drift detection, formal model governance, and security/compliance implementation.
