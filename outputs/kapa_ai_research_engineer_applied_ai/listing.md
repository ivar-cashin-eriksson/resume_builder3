# Research Engineer, Applied AI

https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/kapa-ai/jobs/huRVu7n-research-engineer-applied-ai

## Overview

kapa.ai builds AI assistants for technical content. The role sits on the applied research side of that product: improving the system so it can answer harder technical questions reliably and at production quality.

The company frames the work around technical documentation, grounded answers, and evaluation-driven iteration. The public product pages describe an answer engine designed for production use, with grounded answers and citations, and the YC listing describes work on RAG evaluation, benchmark creation, agentic retrieval, and embedding or rerank model tuning.

## Practical Signals From The Listing

- Research engineering applied to a production AI product.
- Retrieval and RAG evaluation without labelled data.
- Benchmark design and experimental iteration.
- Agentic retrieval that chooses between fast and slower paths.
- Embedding or reranker fine-tuning.
- Work with the founding team and software engineers.
- Applied ML work in an environment where things are loosely defined.
- Europe remote, with a Copenhagen office.
