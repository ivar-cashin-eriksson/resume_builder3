# Job Analysis: OpenAI AI Deployment Engineer, Startups

## Main Role Signals

- Startup-facing technical deployment role.
- Close work with highly technical customers.
- Translation of messy customer workflows into clear technical signals.
- Prompt, agent, and workflow prototyping.
- Evaluation and benchmark design from real usage patterns.
- Feedback loops into product and research.
- Strong product judgement and ambiguity tolerance.
- Production systems experience.
- Clear communication across technical and non-technical audiences.
- Stockholm location.

## Tailoring Strategy

The CV should emphasise:

- customer-facing ML engineering and consulting;
- production systems;
- ambiguous problem framing and stakeholder translation;
- natural-language-input ML at Valcon;
- full-stack AI application building through Project Iris;
- startup validation and market analysis through Project Iris;
- prompt and agentic-system interest through resume_builder, without overclaiming direct LLM deployment;
- Stockholm and Swedish-language fit;
- strong academic background in ML, statistics, physics, and mathematics.

The CV should avoid claiming:

- direct OpenAI API deployment experience;
- direct work with OpenAI customers, incorporated startups, or funded startup operating companies;
- LLM production serving, prompt engineering as paid work, agent product ownership, or formal eval systems;
- reinforcement learning beyond coursework unless phrased as education.
