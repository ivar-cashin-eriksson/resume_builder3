## Listing Summary

The AI Deployment Engineering team works closely with frontier startups. The team acts as trusted technical advisors and thought partners, helping startups deploy OpenAI technology safely and effectively, while turning customer learnings into evaluation systems, product improvements, and better model behaviour.

The role sits between customer reality and model quality. It combines hands-on technical depth, product judgement, workflow debugging, prompt and agent iteration, evaluation design, and collaboration with research and product teams.

## Responsibilities

- Work directly with strategic startup customers to understand workflows, uncover failure modes, and identify high-impact opportunities.
- Prototype and iterate on prompts, agents, and workflow designs.
- Turn usage patterns into reproducible evaluations, benchmarks, and technical artefacts for product and research teams.
- Build repeatable tools, patterns, and evaluation approaches across multiple use cases.
- Operate with strong judgement in ambiguous environments.
- Build relationships within the startup ecosystem as a technical partner.

## Candidate Signals

- Strong software engineering and AI fundamentals.
- Experience as a software engineer, ML engineer, data scientist, startup CTO, or equivalent.
- Production systems experience is a strong plus.
- Experience with, or interest in, model training pipelines and reinforcement learning.
- Experience building AI applications, agents, or evaluation systems.
- Ability to work directly with technical users and translate their challenges into concrete technical signals.
- Ability to move between prototyping, debugging, evaluation design, and cross-functional collaboration.
- Clear communication across technical and non-technical audiences.
- High agency, product sense, and focus on durable improvements rather than one-off fixes.
