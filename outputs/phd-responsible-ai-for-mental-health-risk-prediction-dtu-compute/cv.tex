\section{Detailed Work Experience}

% ---------------------------
\phantomsection\label{sec:raysearch}
\cventry{2019--2021}{Researcher within Data Science}{RaySearch Laboratories}{Stockholm}{}{
    At RaySearch Laboratories, I worked on automating radiotherapy planning using deep learning, probabilistic modelling, and multi-objective optimisation. I developed pipelines for extracting latent anatomical features from segmented 3D CT scans using autoencoders, and used those representations to identify similar patients via a learned similarity metric.
    \newline\hspace*{2em}
    Dose-volume histograms and spatial dose distributions were then predicted using sparse Gaussian processes, giving a probabilistic framework for dose planning conditioned on patient geometry and a practical way to handle uncertainty in the outputs. I also redesigned lexicographic optimisation algorithms to better reflect clinical decision hierarchies.
    \newline\hspace*{2em}
    Throughout the work, I collaborated closely with medical physicists and clinicians to keep the models aligned with oncological standards and practical workflow requirements.
}{}
