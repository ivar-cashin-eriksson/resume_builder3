## Strong Matches

- Production ML ownership and deployment
- Databricks and cloud-based data platform work
- Python, SQL, ML engineering, and software engineering

## Gaps Or Weaker Evidence

- No explicit example of customer-facing conversational AI delivery
- No explicit production RAG, tool-calling, or agent architecture in the profile
- No explicit MLflow project delivery, even though MLflow appears in the skills list
- No direct experience in fintech or payments
- No explicit document processing or audio processing example

## Implication For The Draft

- The CV and cover letter should stay close to production ML, embeddings, multimodal work, and ownership.
- The draft can safely foreground Python, SQL, Databricks, MLflow, AWS, and Azure as part of the documented toolset.
- The draft should not claim direct experience with conversational AI products unless that is supported elsewhere.
- The wording should frame Flatpay as an adjacent step from current work, not as a perfect one-to-one match.
